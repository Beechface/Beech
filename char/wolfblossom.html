Wolfblossom is tall, with broad shoulders and a sturdy build very much like her parents. Her fur is long, plush and thick. A opalescent off-white base, she has lilac tabby markings across her head and tail, with white muzzle and paws and a striking pink nose. Her eyes are pale blue. Like her mother, she carries the well-built, muscular haunches of a Skyclan cat.

Outgoing and friendly, with a soft spot for kits and apprentices. Her easygoing, somewhat clumsy nature hides a sharp, cunning mind. Wolfblossom is slow to anger, but is fiercely protective of her clan. Will bully her siblings and fellow clanmates into taking care of themselves. Not a pushover. Very deliberate and observant, despite the outgoing and friendly demeanor.
