Brackenwing is a sleek-furred marbled lynx point like her father Bearspirit. Her fur is short but thick, and soft. A pale cream base is marked with warm, light honey-brown swirls that fade to darker brown points at the face, legs and tail. Her chest and underside are paler in shade. Like her father, she is powerfully built with long legs and tail, and doesn't much resemble the typical Windclan warrior. Her eyes are green.

Bracken is shy and quiet, an introvert in every sense of the word. She's hesitant and deeply conflicted over knowing that she's the product of a forbidden romance, and so is determined to prove her loyalty while also keeping her head down and keeping both herself and her siblings out of trouble. She could easily be seen as the strict, no-nonsense type, though she does have a softer side that not many beyond her family will get to see.
